## FAQ

**Is it really free?**  
Yes. The free tier has no limits. We make money from Pro users who want cloud sync and team features.

**Where is my data stored?**  
On your device. We use localStorage (and soon IndexedDB). Your data never leaves your browser unless you export it.

**Can I use it on mobile?**  
Yes. Install it as a PWA on iOS or Android. It works offline.

**Is there a desktop app?**  
Not yet. The web app works great on desktop. A native app is on the roadmap.
