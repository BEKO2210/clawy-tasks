## Break-Even Analysis

**Costs:**
- Hosting (Vercel): $0 (free tier)
- Domain: $12/year
- Stripe fees: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- Time: ~10 hours/week

**Break-even:**
- At $5/month: 1 Pro user covers domain
- At 50 Pro users: $250/month covers time (opportunity cost)
- At 200 Pro users: $1,000/month = meaningful side income
- At 1,000 Pro users: $5,000/month = full-time income

## Exit Options

**Option 1: Keep as side income**
- $1,000-$5,000/month
- Low maintenance
- Passive income

**Option 2: Sell the product**
- 3-5x ARR valuation
- At $5,000 MRR = $180,000-$300,000 sale
- Find buyer on MicroAcquire, FE International

**Option 3: Grow to full-time**
- Hire support/dev
- Add team features
- Target $50,000 MRR
- Raise prices, add enterprise tier
